What is Popit?

Pop It is a toy in the form of a piece of silicone like bubbles that can be squeezed. This toy also has a variety of different shapes, colors and sizes so it is very interesting for children. This toy is not only entertaining but can also train the motor and sensory skills of early childhood.

Rule of the game

Your little one can press the bubbles on this pop it toy, then the bubbles will pop back up. The way to play is to take turns pressing a predetermined number of bubbles that are on one line. Later each player can press 1 bubble or several bubbles at the same time according to the instructions. Then the player who presses the last bubble loses.
